<h2>XBMC Add-ons:</h2>

<h3><u>After Watch</u></h3>

<blockquote><img src='https://hautopc.googlecode.com/svn/trunk/logos/afterwatch.png' height='128' /></blockquote>

<blockquote><h4>Summary</h4>
After watching a movie or episode:<br>
- Move or delete<br>
- Rate on IMDb or library<br>
- Show recommendations<br>
- More...</blockquote>

<blockquote><h4>Requirements</h4>
<ul><li>XBMC 12<br>
</li><li>Internet connection (optional)<br>
</li><li>Any OS</li></ul></blockquote>

<blockquote><h4>Download</h4>
<a href='https://hautopc.googlecode.com/files/xbmc.repository.hautopc.zip'>https://hautopc.googlecode.com/files/xbmc.repository.hautopc.zip</a></blockquote>

<blockquote><h4>Setup</h4>
<ul><li>Download repository above<br>
</li><li>In XBMC go to System > Settings > Add-ons > Install from zip file<br>
</li><li>Navigate to where you downloaded repository and select it<br>
</li><li>Go to Get Addo-ons > HauToPC Add-ons > Services > After Watch > Install<br>
</li><li>Wait a little bit until notification shows<br>
</li><li>Return to XBMC home and go to Programs > After Watch<br>
</li><li>Modify the settings to your needs<br>
</li><li>Watch a movie or episode in your library!</li></ul></blockquote>

<h2>uTorrent Scripts:</h2>

<h3><u>Tor Finisher</u></h3>

<blockquote><img src='https://hautopc.googlecode.com/svn/trunk/logos/torfinisher.png' height='128' /></blockquote>

<blockquote><h4>Summary</h4>
When a movie or episode download finishes, automaticaly unrar or copy the video file to your library, with all proper structure and naming. Also creates a nfo file with imdb url (if movie, for your XBMC scrapper). Then it will send you an email notifying you of new things to watch.<br>
Plus, it refreshes your XBMC library to add the downloaded media, and deletes torrents based on some conditions when the disks are full. This way you will never need to handle downloads or files again, just turn on your TV and see whats new to watch.</blockquote>

<blockquote><a href='http://www.youtube.com/watch?feature=player_embedded&v=wVFfbol9s8Y' target='_blank'><img src='http://img.youtube.com/vi/wVFfbol9s8Y/0.jpg' width='425' height=344 /></a></blockquote>

<blockquote><h4>Requirements</h4>
<ul><li>Windows OS<br>
</li><li>uTorrent 3<br>
</li><li>Winrar<br>
</li><li>XBMC 12 (optional)<br>
</li><li>Internet connection</li></ul></blockquote>

<blockquote><h4>Download</h4>
<a href='https://hautopc.googlecode.com/svn/trunk/downloads/TorGrabberSetup-1.1.2.exe'>https://hautopc.googlecode.com/svn/trunk/downloads/TorGrabberSetup-1.1.2.exe</a></blockquote>

<blockquote><h4>Setup</h4>
<ul><li>Download installer above, run the GUI and configure it<br>
</li><li>In Settings dont forget to click 'Configure run when finish'<br>
</li><li>Configure uTorrent RSS Downloader with labels for movies and tv shows (eg: MOVIES, SERIES/{TITLE IN FOLDER})<br>
</li><li>(optional) Open XBMC and go to System > Settings > Services > Webserver, enable Allow Control of XBMC via HTTP and set port/username/password. Also, in Services > Remote Control, enable both<br>
</li><li>(optional) Set up a Gmail account to send you notifications of new media<br>
</li><li>(optional) set utorrent move_to_trash in advanced settings to false<br>
</li><li>Wait for a torrent to finish!</li></ul></blockquote>
